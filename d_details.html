Savanna Rose Arts

 

Drawing is a huge outlet for me, and in many ways it is my most practiced medium. It is much easier for me to pull out pencils or markers than it is to pull out my paintings supplies, so thus, i find myself drawing most often. My favorite thing about drawing is it gives you so much room to play because of all the different materials and mediums that can go into a single piece.
